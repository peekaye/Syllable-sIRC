Workspaces

Syllable has a neat feature called workspaces, which you can think of as twelve different desktops, all working at the same time.

You're already using workspaces! When you log in to Syllable, you're automatically placed in workspace 1, and eleven other workspaces are ready for your use.

To access each workspace, press and hold the Alt key, then press one of the function keys at the top of your keyboard. To go to workspace two, press and hold Alt, then press F2. For workspace ten, press Alt F10. To return to workspace one, press Alt F1.

How are workspaces useful? Imagine having your web browser open on workspace 1, and a word processor open on workspace 2. While you're writing your novel on workspace 2, you can immediately switch to your web browser with a quick press of Alt F1.
