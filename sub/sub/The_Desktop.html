The Desktop

The icons on the desktop are just a collection of shortcuts (in Unix terms, symlinks) to various folders and applications on your Syllable system.

These shortcuts all live in the Desktop folder in your Home folder. To add an icon to your desktop, just create a symlink by typing the following in a terminal window: 

ln -s <application> ~/Desktop/<new icon name>

So, to create an icon for Whisper on your desktop, type the following: 

ln -s /Applications/Whisper/bin/Whisper ~/Desktop/Whisper
