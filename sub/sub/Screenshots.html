Taking a Screenshot
Written by:Brent P. Newhall
Last Updated:Apr 11, 2007
Version:0.3.4 to 0.5.3

To take a screenshot in Syllable, press the Printscreen key. A PNG image file starting with the word "Screenshot", followed by the date and time, will be created in the /tmp/ directory.

Note that, currently, you can't take any screenshots if your screen is set to 16-bit mode (which is the default). In Preferences, open the Screen preferences window, and change the mode to 15-bit or 32-bit.
