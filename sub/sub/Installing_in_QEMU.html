Installing in QEMU
Written by:AgentM
Last Updated:Apr 11, 2007
Version:0.5.1 to 0.5.3

Installing in qemu is exactly as installing from a basic or premium cd, with one exception: you MUST turn off ata dma. To do that:

  1. At the grub menu, press the E key.
  2. Press the down arrow once until the kernel=... line is highlighted.
  3. Press the E key again.
  4. Type: enable_ata_dma=false
  5. Press the Enter key.
  6. Press the Esc key.
  7. Press the B key to start the boot process.

When the installer asks if you want to view, edit, or install the menu.lst, edit it and disable DMA.
