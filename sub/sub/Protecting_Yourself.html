Protecting Yourself

As stable and wonderful as Syllable is, it's still possible for bad things to happen. So, there are some things you can do to prevent those bad things from causing serious damage when they do happen.

One neat trick used by at least one Syllable developer is to create a whole separate partition just for your home directory, and mount it. That way, if AFS crashes on your system, at least your home directory will still be okay.

Oh, and run "sync" from the terminal often.
